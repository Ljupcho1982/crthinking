FAQ

Questions, answered

Will my data stay private?+

Yes — that's the whole point. CRThinking specialises in local and on-prem AI. Models can run fully offline on your own hardware, so nothing has to leave the building.

Do I need expensive GPUs or cloud?+

Often not. We fine-tune and quantize models to run on modest machines — even laptops — and only reach for the cloud when it genuinely earns its place.

What languages can the AI speak?+

Multilingual by design, including Macedonian. Voice and text agents are tuned to your customers' actual language and tone.

Do I own the result?+

Completely. You keep the weights, the source code and the data. No vendor lock-in, no per-seat AI tax.

How fast can we start?+

A focused prototype can prove value in days. Email or call and we'll scope it honestly — including whether an LLM is even the right tool.
